Jeffrey Alido

I'm a final year PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Boston University with Professor Lei Tian in the Computational Imaging Systems Lab. My research focuses on computational imaging, imaging inverse problems, and generative modeling. I have done machine learning and software engineering internships at insitro and Google. I received my B.S. from UC San Diego and I'm a recipient of the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Whitened Score Diffusion: A Structured Prior for Imaging Inverse Problems
Jeffrey Alido, Tongyu Li, Yu Sun, Lei Tian
arXiv, 2025
arXiv / code

We propose Whitened Score diffusion models that learn the Whitened Score function instead of the standard score, enabling stable training on arbitrary Gaussian forward noising processes and providing strong Bayesian priors for imaging inverse problems with structured noise.
